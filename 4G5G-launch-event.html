EVENT INFO

As a global leading provider of professional communications technologies and solutions, Hytera has 27 years' wireless experience with vertical and industrial customers. We have built more than 4,000 private networks serving over 15 Million professional users, now our latest 4G/5G product and solutions are ready.

We would like to invite you to join Hytera's 4G & 5G systems and products launch event, which will be held online on 29 June, 14:00 (UTC/GMT+2). Hytera will bring insights and expertise in professional mobile communications and actively cooperates with ecosystem partners to empower the vertical industries' evolution to digital, broadband, and 5G. You will also get to know more about Hytera's industry-leading solutions including DU board, O-BBU and high-end Edge-Node.
